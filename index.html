Tomi Kauppinen is an author, podcast host, keynote speaker, information visualizer, cyber-physical learning designer & cognitive systems researcher in the Helsinki area. Tomi is Head of Aalto Online Learning that aspires to develop, explore and evaluate a variety of online and blended approaches for teaching and learning at Aalto University.

He holds venia legendi (habilitation, 2014) in geoinformatics from the University of Muenster (WWU) in Germany, and a title of docent (2014) and a Ph.D. (2010) in media technology from the Aalto University.
